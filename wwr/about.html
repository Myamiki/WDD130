History



**History of Rapids & Rides Rafting Co.** Rapids & Rides Rafting Co. was founded in 1998 by a group of adventurous friends with a shared passion for exploring the untamed rivers of the Rocky Mountains. What began as weekend rafting trips on local rivers quickly turned into a business idea when the founders realized how eager people were to experience the thrill of whitewater rafting. The founders, each with years of experience in outdoor leadership and adventure sports, decided to take the plunge and establish a company that would combine their love for nature, adventure, and customer service. In the early years, the company operated with just two inflatable rafts and a small van that transported guests to the starting points. Word spread quickly about the quality of the rafting experience Rapids & Rides offered, and soon the company had to expand to meet demand. By the mid-2000s, it had grown from a small local outfit into a regional leader in whitewater rafting, offering a variety of adventure packages for families, adrenaline junkies, and corporate team-building events alike.

As Rapids & Rides continued to grow, so did their commitment to environmental sustainability and community involvement. The company has always prioritized giving back to the rivers and the natural landscapes that make the rafting experience possible. Through partnerships with environmental organizations, Rapids & Rides participates in annual river clean-up events and supports conservation efforts to maintain healthy river ecosystems. Over the years, the company has also focused on safety, becoming a leader in wilderness first aid training and swift water rescue techniques for both their guides and guests. Today, Rapids & Rides Rafting Co. operates across five major river systems in the United States, serving over 20,000 adventurers each year. From the early days of braving the rapids with a handful of friends to becoming a premier rafting company, the journey of Rapids & Rides is one filled with excitement, growth, and a deep respect for nature.
